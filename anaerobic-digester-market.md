<p><p>The anaerobic digester market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Upflow Anaerobic Sludge Blanket (UASB)** employs a rising flow of wastewater, facilitating efficient sludge retention and biogas production. </p><p>2. **Internal Circulation Reactor (IC Reactor)** enhances mixing and contact between wastewater and sludge, increasing treatment efficiency.</p><p>3. **Expanded Granular Sludge Bed Digestion (EGSB)** optimizes biogas yield by allowing for higher flow rates through expanded granular sludge. </p><p>4. **Others** encompass various innovative anaerobic digestion technologies and hybrid systems, catering to specific treatment needs and applications.</p></p>
