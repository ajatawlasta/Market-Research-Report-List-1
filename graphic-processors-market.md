<p><p>The graphic processors market is primarily divided into two types: discrete and integrated graphics. Discrete graphics processors are standalone units that provide superior performance for demanding tasks like gaming and 3D rendering. They are favored by enthusiasts and professionals. In contrast, integrated graphics are built into the CPU, offering a more compact solution suited for everyday tasks and general computing, often found in budget devices. Each type caters to different user needs and hardware configurations in the market.</p></p>
