<p><p>Radial piston hydraulic motors are categorized into two main types based on displacement: multiple displacement and single displacement. Multiple displacement motors can adjust the volume of fluid displaced per revolution, allowing for variable speed and torque options, making them versatile for different applications. In contrast, single displacement motors have a fixed displacement, providing consistent performance, but less adaptability. Both types are widely used in various industries, including construction and agriculture, for their efficiency in converting hydraulic energy into mechanical power.</p></p>
