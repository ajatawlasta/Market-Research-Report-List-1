<p><p>The industrial refractory magnesia market comprises various types, including fused magnesia, dead burned magnesia, and others. Fused magnesia is produced by melting high-purity magnesite, offering superior clarity and performance in high-temperature applications. Dead burned magnesia is created by sintering magnesite at high temperatures, ideal for refractory bricks and molds due to its thermal stability. Other types may include caustic calcined magnesia and magnesium hydroxide, used in various industrial applications, including construction and chemical processes.</p></p>
