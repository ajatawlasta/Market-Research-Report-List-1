<p><p>Eddy current separators are devices used to separate non-ferrous metals from other materials using induced electrical currents. The market for these separators can be classified into two main types: concentric rotor and eccentric rotor eddy current separators. Concentric rotor separators feature a uniform magnetic field, providing consistent separation performance, while eccentric rotor separators have a rotating magnetic field that enhances separation efficiency, particularly for lighter materials. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific recycling and waste management applications.</p></p>
