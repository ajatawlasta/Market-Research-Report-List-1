<p><ul><li>Direct Probes</li><li>Indirect Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitance measurement probes market consists of two primary types: direct probes and indirect probes. Direct probes offer precise capacitance measurements by placing the probe directly in contact with the device under test, ensuring accurate readings. In contrast, indirect probes utilize electromagnetic fields to measure capacitance without direct contact, making them suitable for sensitive or inaccessible components. Both types cater to different testing requirements across various industries, including electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, facilitating effective circuit analysis and quality control.</p></p>
