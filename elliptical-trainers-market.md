<p><strong>The Elliptical Trainers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Drive</li><li>Rear Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elliptical trainers come in three main market types: front drive, rear drive, and center drive. Front drive ellipticals feature the flywheel at the front, allowing for a more natural stride and often producing a longer, more linear movement. Rear drive models place the flywheel at the back, promoting a fluid motion but generally taking up more space. Center drive ellipticals have the flywheel positioned in the center, offering a compact design and a more stable, balanced workout experience.</p></p>
