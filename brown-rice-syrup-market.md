<p><p>Brown rice syrup is a natural sweetener derived from brown rice through enzymatic processing, which breaks down the starches into sugars. It is commonly used as an alternative to refined sugars and artificial sweeteners in various food and beverage products, including baked goods, cereals, and energy bars. Known for its mild, nutty flavor and low glycemic index, it is often favored by health-conscious consumers and those seeking gluten-free or vegan options.</p><p>The Brown Rice Syrup Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period.
