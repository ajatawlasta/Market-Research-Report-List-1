<p><p>The automotive camera module adhesives market consists of two primary types: UV curable and non-UV curable adhesives. </p><p>UV curable adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to initiate curing, offering rapid bonding and enhanced precision, suitable for high-volume production in automotive applications. In contrast, non-UV curable adhesives rely on chemical reactions for curing, providing versatility in application but typically requiring longer setting times. Both types are essential for ensuring durable assembly and reliable performance of automotive camera systems, enhancing vehicle safety and advanced driver-assistance features.</p></p>
