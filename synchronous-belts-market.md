<p><p>Synchronous belts, also known as timing belts, are toothed belts used to transmit power efficiently between rotating shafts. They are widely used in automotive, industrial machinery, and robotics due to their precision, low maintenance, and ability to operate without slippage. These belts are designed with teeth that mesh with corresponding grooves in pulleys, ensuring synchronized motion and high torque transmission.</p><p>The Synchronous Belts Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand across industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and renewable energy.
