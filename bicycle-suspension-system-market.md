<p><p>The bicycle suspension system market is primarily divided into two types: hardtail suspension and full suspension. Hardtail bikes feature a rigid rear end with front suspension forks, offering simplicity and efficiency, making them ideal for cross-country riding. In contrast, full suspension bikes incorporate both front and rear suspension systems, enhancing comfort and control on rough terrains, suitable for mountain biking and aggressive trails. Each type addresses different rider preferences and terrain challenges, driving diverse market demand.</p></p>
