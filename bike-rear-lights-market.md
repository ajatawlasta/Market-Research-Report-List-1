<p><strong>The Bike Rear Lights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Generating Lights</li><li>Battery Powered Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bike rear lights market includes two primary types: self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Self-generating lights rely on a dynamo system that converts pedaling motion into energy, eliminating the need for batteries and ensuring consistent brightness. These are often eco-friendly and low-maintenance. Conversely, battery-powered lights run on replaceable or rechargeable batteries, offering flexibility in design and brightness levels but requiring regular maintenance to replace or recharge. Both types enhance visibility and safety for cyclists, especially in low-light conditions.</p></p>
