<p><ul><li>Resin Type</li><li>Rubber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor release agent market encompasses two primary types: resin-based and rubber-based agents. Resin type release agents are often formulated with thermosetting polymers, providing excellent adhesion resistance and allowing for easy component removal during manufacturing. They are suited for high-temperature applications. In contrast, rubber type release agents utilize elastomeric compounds that offer flexibility and enhanced surface protection. These agents are typically favored for their durability and effectiveness in various molding processes, catering to different operational requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
