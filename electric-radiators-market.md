<p><p>The electric radiators market includes various types designed for efficient heating solutions. Fan heaters provide rapid warmth through hot air circulation, ideal for instant heating. Convection radiators warm the air directly, creating a comfortable ambient temperature. Infrared radiators emit radiant heat, warming objects and people directly, making them suitable for spot heating. Oil-based electric radiators use heated oil to retain warmth, offering sustained comfort and energy efficiency, perfect for long-term heating needs. Each type caters to different preferences and applications.</p></p>
