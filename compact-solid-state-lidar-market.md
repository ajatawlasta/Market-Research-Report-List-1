<p><strong>The Compact Solid-state LIDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phased Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compact Solid-state LIDAR market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **MEMS Based Scanning**: Utilizes Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems to rotate a laser beam, enabling effective distance measurement while maintaining a small form factor.</p><p>2. **Phased Array**: Employs multiple fixed lasers and sensors that adjust the phase to steer the beam electronically, allowing for rapid scanning without mechanical movement.</p><p>3. **Non-Scanning Flash**: Emits a wide-angle light pulse, capturing distance data in a single shot, ideal for applications requiring immediate and comprehensive range information.</p></p>
