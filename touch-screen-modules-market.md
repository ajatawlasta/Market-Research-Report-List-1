<p><strong>The Touch Screen Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Type</li><li>Capacitive Induction Type</li><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Surface Acoustic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen modules market encompasses several types, each with distinct technologies. Resistive type screens detect touch through pressure applied to two conductive layers. Capacitive induction type screens sense touch through electrical properties of the human body, offering enhanced sensitivity and clarity. Infrared type uses an array of infrared sensors to detect touch, providing high durability and clarity. Surface acoustic type screens utilize ultrasonic waves, detecting touch based on disruption, resulting in high image quality and responsiveness. Each type serves different application needs.</p></p>
