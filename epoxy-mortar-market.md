<p><p>The epoxy mortar market primarily consists of two types: two-component and three-component epoxy mortars. Two-component epoxy mortars comprise a resin and a hardener that are mixed before application, offering strong adhesion and durability. In contrast, three-component epoxy mortars include an additional additive, enhancing properties such as flexibility, chemical resistance, and workability. Both types are widely used in construction and industrial applications due to their robust performance, making them ideal for flooring, repair, and protective coatings.</p></p>
