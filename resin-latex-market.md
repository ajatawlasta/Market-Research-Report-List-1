<p><p>The resin latex market comprises **Natural Rubber Latex (NRL)** and **Synthetic Rubber Latex (SRL)**. NRL is derived from rubber trees and is valued for its elasticity, biodegradability, and use in gloves, adhesives, and foam products. SRL, produced from petroleum-based chemicals, offers tailored properties like chemical resistance and durability, making it ideal for industrial coatings, textiles, and automotive applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, with NRL being eco-friendly and SRL providing versatility and performance in specialized applications.</p></p>
